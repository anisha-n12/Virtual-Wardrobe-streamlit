About VirtuaDress AI

VirtuaDress AI was created to bridge the gap between online shopping and the physical fitting room experience. We leverage the power of cutting-edge diffusion models, particularly those based on the VTON (Virtual Try-On) framework, to provide users with realistic visualizations of clothing items on a person.

Our backend is built on **Flask** for reliability and speed, and the complex image generation task is delegated to powerful, open-source AI models hosted on platforms like Hugging Face Spaces (via the **Gradio Client API**). This allows us to focus on user experience while providing access to state-of-the-art technology.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to make confident fashion decisions by providing a seamless, stable, and accessible virtual try-on experience directly from their browser.
